Help users to

Access a service: with personal details

Also known as Authenticate or Identifying Users

Recommended

This pattern is currently experimental because more research is needed to validate it. 

Help users to authenticate themselves so they can access a service in a secure way with personal details.

When to use this pattern

Follow this pattern if you are helping users access a service by authenticating users with personal details.

Services using this pattern

  • Access UK
  • Apply for the EU Settlement Scheme
  • Status: right to rent, right to work, view & prove
  • Update your EU Settlement Scheme details

Research

More research is needed on the best way to help users access a service by authenticating them with personal details. If your service helps users authenticate themselves with personal details, get in touch to share your user research findings.

Get in touch

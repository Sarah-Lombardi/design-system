Allow users to submit an enquiry to a service.

Your enquiry

Your details so we can contact you

The information you give will be emailed to our customer resolution team. Your data will be deleted once the case is resolved.

When to use this pattern

Follow this pattern:

  • whenever you need to include an enquiry form in your service
  • to link to in follow-up communications with users

When not to use this pattern

Do not follow this pattern:

  • as a feedback form - you should use a GOV.UK feedback or 'done' page at the end of a service

How it works

It can be used:

  • with a variety of enquiry and support types
  • with optional contact details

It can include:

  • information on how the service will store and use the information users give
  • a list of options for the subject of the enquiry

Research

More research is needed. If your service uses this pattern, get in touch to share your user research findings.

Accessibility

If your service uses this pattern, let us know of any insights you have on accessibility considerations.

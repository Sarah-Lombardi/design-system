# Time

Displays a time in accordance with Gov style guidance.

## How to use this component.
### Standard

Display the time in the 12 hour clock.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Standard">
	<Time
	  time="22:37"
	/>
  </Story>
</Preview>

### 24 Hour

Display the time in the 24 hour clock.

<Preview>
  <Story name="24 Hour">
	<Time
	  time="22:37"
	  clockType={24}
	/>
  </Story>
</Preview>


### With date-time

If it is convenient, you can provide a full date-time and display just the time as calculated from the timezone.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Date-time">
	  <Time
	    time="2022-07-21T22:37:34.570Z"
	  />
  </Story>
</Preview>
